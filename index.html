About

Let's get a little personal!

  • Interests & Hobbies

    Outside of coding, I enjoy a variety of things. I like to watch anime, learn new recipes, learn new languages (computer or otherwise), and play video games! Recently, I've also taken an interest in building computers!

  • Start of My Journey

    I started my journey into the world of programming in 2020 when I began studying Python on my own. After building a small hangman-style program with list indexing, I decided to pursue software engineering further by joining App Academy!

  • Time At App Academy

    After a rigorous 16-week program, I graduated from App Academy in 2024. I learned a variety of technologies and languages such as JavaScript, React, Redux, and PostgreSQL. I also gained more insight into Python and learned Flask. I also learned how to work with others in a team environment and how to build full-stack applications.

  • Goals & Future Plans

    On this journey, I hope to continue to learn and grow as a software engineer. I hope to work with a team of like-minded individuals to complete our goals and connect as a team. I also hope to learn more about cybersecurity and how to protect the applications I build. I also want begin learning to build games as a side hobby or even as a career! I plan to learn as many languages as I can to continue to grow as a programmer.

WindRnR

An AirBnB clone for those who need a place wherever the wind takes them.

This is a full-stack program built using an Express.js backend and a React frontend utilizing Redux. It was the first project I ever made and is an AirBnB clone. This was my first experience with learning how the two work together.

FundMyJunk

Where Philanthropy and Terrible Ideas Meet

FundMyJunk is a full-stack application that utilizes a Flask backend, React frontend, and PostgreSQL as the database. It takes inspiration from sites such as Kickstater and GoFundMe.
